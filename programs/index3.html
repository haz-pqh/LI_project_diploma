BACHELOR OF ART IN 3D ANIMATION AND DIGITAL MEDIA (HONOURS)

Designed to equip students with the creative and technical skills needed to excel in the fields of 3D animation, digital media, and multimedia production.

About

Who we are

The program equips students with practical experience in 2D, 3D, and stop-motion animation for film, television, visual effects, and games, while also offering specialization in areas like character animation, production design, or story development. It blends hands-on training with theoretical knowledge, covering the history, theory, and narrative structures of animation. Graduates will be well-prepared for careers as animators, production designers, directors, and more, providing a comprehensive education in both the creative and technical aspects of the animation industry.
