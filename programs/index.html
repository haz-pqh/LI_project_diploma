FOUNDATION IN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (FT001)

Designed to provide students with a strong foundation in the fundamentals of IT.

About

Who we are

This programme was developed to provide the students with firm knowledge and understanding of the latest ICT dynamics and commercial environments. The programme is intended to provide strong fundamentals in information technology that can support the student in tertiary study. This foundation study is also accredited by the Malaysian Qualifications Agency (MQA).


  • Programme : Foundation in Information Technology
  • Programme Code : FT001
  • Medium of Instruction : English
  • Reference : R/010/3/0483 (MQA/FA9041) 15/09/2022-14/09/2027
  • Tuition fee : RM11,925

Minimum Requirement :

  • A pass in Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM)/ Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia Vokasional (SPMV) with at least credit in FIVE (5) subjects including Mathematics.

    • OR


  • A pass in Unified Examination Certificate (UEC) with at least grade B in THREE (3) subjects.

    • OR


  • A pass in O-Level with at least grade C in FIVE (5) subjects.

    • OR


  • Other equivalent qualifications recognised by the Malaysian Government.
