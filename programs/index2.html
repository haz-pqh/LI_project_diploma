DIPLOMA IN COMPUTER SCIENCE (CC101)

Designed to provide students with a strong foundation in the fundamentals of IT.

About

Who we are

The Diploma in Computer Science at Universiti Poly-Tech Malaysia (UPTM) provides students with a strong foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of computing. The program covers essential skills such as the use of ICT tools, software development techniques, and the analysis of technical information. Graduates will be well-versed in the latest technologies and equipped with effective communication skills, making them highly employable in various industries. This diploma prepares students for successful careers by blending academic knowledge with practical, workplace-relevant skills.

Minimum Requirement :

  • A pass in Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM) or equivalent with at least credit in any THREE (3) subjects inclusive of Mathematics or any equivalent qualification.

    • OR


  • A pass in Sijil Kemahiran Malaysia (SKM) Level 3 in related field and a credit in Mathematics at SPM level.

    • OR


  • A pass in any qualifications equivalent to certificates (Level 3, MQF) and a credit in Mathematics at SPM level.

    • OR


  • A pass in Sijil Tinggi Persekolahan Malaysia (STPM) with a minimum of grade C (GP 2.0) in any subjects or any equivalent qualification and a credit in Mathematics at SPM level.

    • OR


  • A pass in Sijil Tinggi Agama Malaysia (STAM) with a minimum grade of Maqbul (pass) and a credit in Mathematics at SPM level or its equivalent.

Notes : Candidates with no credit in Mathematics at SPM level can be admitted if the certificate program contains Mathematics equivalent to Mathematics at the SPM level.

Notes : Candidates with credit in any computing related subject at SPM level or its equivalent may be given preferential consideration.
