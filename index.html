WELCOME SPEECH

The Faculty of Computing and Multimedia (FCOM) at Universiti Poly-Tech Malaysia is dedicated to academic excellence, offering outstanding teaching and research programs with 40 highly qualified academic staff. We provide six undergraduate and two postgraduate programs, designed to equip students with the skills needed for employment, further training, or entrepreneurship. Our courses address real-world problems and are guided by experienced lecturers, ensuring students graduate as skilled IT professionals ready to impact the world positively. We are proud of our high graduate employability and continuous development, welcoming new students to join our vibrant academic community.
